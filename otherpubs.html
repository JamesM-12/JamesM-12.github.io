U.S. Geological Survey Publications

Publications relevent to the geology of South Carolina.

Overview

Digital and physical copies of other publications may be available from the S.C. Geological Survey or the U.S. Geological Survey. Prices are listed for hard copies available from the S.C. Geological Survey and can be purchased by filling out an order form here. Links have been provided for digital formats available from the U.S. Geological Survey. U.S. Geological Survey Open-file reports are not intended to be final publications. Most of the OFRs listed have been superseded by more formal publications, and they are not available at the S.C. Geological Survey. However they are relevant to South Carolina geology, and they are listed here.
